Animating the Winnebago Walleye Study (This site is not affiliated with the WI DNR.)

With the help of local fishing clubs, the WI DNR did a really cool study of walleye movments on Lake Winnebago from 2011 to 2013. They inserted sonic tags into 100 walleyes and set up devices to receive the signal emitted from these fish at various locations around the Lake Winnebago system. They produced a very informative report here: Study Report

The goal of this website is to help visualize the data generated from this study.
